Account Deletion

Schulte Vision Trainer account deletion instructions

Users can delete their account directly within the Schulte Vision Trainer application.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open Schulte Vision Trainer.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion.

Deletion is processed immediately.

What Is Deleted

  • User account.
  • Premium status.
  • Subscription verification data.
  • User profile information.

What Is Retained

  • Data required by law, if applicable.

Local Data on Your Device

Training statistics and session history are stored locally on the user's device and are removed when the application data is deleted or the application is uninstalled.
